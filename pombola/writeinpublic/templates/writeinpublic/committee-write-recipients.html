Write to a Parliamentary Committee

The most important work of Parliament takes place in its committees. There is a parliamentary committee responsible for overseeing each government department. Write to a committee if you want to bring attention to the poor service delivery or spending of a specific department. You can also lobby the committee to introduce or change a law that deals with your concerns.
